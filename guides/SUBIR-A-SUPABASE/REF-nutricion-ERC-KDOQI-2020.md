# Referencia nutricional — ERC (KDOQI 2020) — DECLARACIONES TEXTUALES

Fuente: KDOQI Clinical Practice Guideline for Nutrition in CKD: 2020 Update.
Ikizler TA, et al; KDOQI Nutrition in CKD Guideline Work Group.
Am J Kidney Dis. 2020;76(3)(suppl 1):S1-S107.

NATURALEZA DE ESTE DOCUMENTO: contiene las **declaraciones de recomendación
citadas de forma textual** (en inglés, como aparecen en la guía original), cada
una seguida de una traducción al español marcada como [ES]. La traducción es una
ayuda de lectura; en caso de duda, el texto en inglés es el autoritativo. El código
entre paréntesis es el nivel de evidencia de la guía (1 = recommend/fuerte,
2 = suggest/sugerencia; A–D calidad; OPINION = consenso). "CKD 1-5" = sin diálisis;
"5D" = en diálisis (MHD hemodiálisis, PD diálisis peritoneal).

## Guideline 3: Protein and Energy Intake

**3.0.1** "In adults with CKD 3-5 who are metabolically stable, we recommend, under
close clinical supervision, protein restriction with or without keto acid analogs, to
reduce risk for end-stage kidney disease (ESKD)/death (1A) and improve quality of life
(QoL) (2C):
- a low-protein diet providing 0.55–0.60 g dietary protein/kg body weight/day, or
- a very low-protein diet providing 0.28–0.43 g dietary protein/kg body weight/day with
  additional keto acid/amino acid analogs to meet protein requirements (0.55–0.60 g/kg
  body weight/day)"
[ES] En adultos con ERC 3-5 metabólicamente estables, se recomienda, bajo supervisión
clínica estrecha, restricción proteica con o sin cetoanálogos: dieta baja en proteína
de 0.55–0.60 g/kg/día, o dieta muy baja en proteína de 0.28–0.43 g/kg/día con
cetoanálogos/aminoácidos hasta cubrir 0.55–0.60 g/kg/día.

**3.0.2** "In the adult with CKD 3-5 and who has diabetes, it is reasonable to prescribe,
under close clinical supervision, a dietary protein intake of 0.6-0.8 g/kg body weight
per day to maintain a stable nutritional status and optimize glycemic control (OPINION)."
[ES] En adulto con ERC 3-5 y diabetes, es razonable prescribir 0.6-0.8 g/kg/día de
proteína, bajo supervisión clínica estrecha, para mantener estado nutricional estable
y optimizar el control glucémico.

**3.0.3** "In adults with CKD 5D on MHD (1C) or PD (OPINION) who are metabolically stable,
we recommend prescribing a dietary protein intake of 1.0-1.2 g/kg body weight per day to
maintain a stable nutritional status."
[ES] En adultos con ERC 5D en hemodiálisis (1C) o diálisis peritoneal (OPINION),
metabólicamente estables, se recomienda 1.0-1.2 g/kg/día de proteína.

**3.0.4** "In adults with CKD 5D and who have diabetes, it is reasonable to prescribe a
dietary protein intake of 1.0-1.2 g/kg body weight per day to maintain a stable
nutritional status. For patients at risk of hyper- and/or hypoglycemia, higher levels of
dietary protein intake may need to be considered to maintain glycemic control (OPINION)."
[ES] En ERC 5D con diabetes, es razonable 1.0-1.2 g/kg/día; en riesgo de hiper/hipoglucemia
puede requerirse mayor aporte proteico.

**3.1.1** "In adults with CKD 1-5D (1C) or posttransplantation (OPINION) who are
metabolically stable, we recommend prescribing an energy intake of 25-35 kcal/kg body
weight per day based on age, sex, level of physical activity, body composition, weight
status goals, CKD stage, and concurrent illness or presence of inflammation to maintain
normal nutritional status."
[ES] En ERC 1-5D o postrasplante, metabólicamente estables, se recomienda 25-35 kcal/kg/día
según edad, sexo, actividad física, composición corporal, objetivos de peso, estadio e
inflamación.

**3.2.1** "In adults with CKD 1-5D (1B) or posttransplantation (OPINION), there is
insufficient evidence to recommend a particular protein type (plant vs animal) in terms
of the effects on nutritional status, calcium or phosphorus levels, or the blood lipid
profile."
[ES] Evidencia insuficiente para recomendar un tipo de proteína (vegetal vs animal).

**3.3.1** "In adults with CKD 1-5 not on dialysis or posttransplantation, with or without
dyslipidemia, we suggest that prescribing a Mediterranean Diet may improve lipid profiles (2C)."
[ES] En ERC 1-5 sin diálisis o postrasplante, una dieta mediterránea puede mejorar el
perfil lipídico.

**3.3.2** "In adults with CKD 1-4, we suggest that prescribing increased fruit and
vegetable intake may decrease body weight, blood pressure, and net acid production (NEAP) (2C)."
[ES] En ERC 1-4, aumentar frutas y verduras puede reducir peso, presión arterial y carga ácida.

## Guideline 6: Electrolytes (textual)

**6.1.3** "In adults with CKD 3-5D, it is reasonable to maintain serum bicarbonate levels
at 24-26 mmol/L (OPINION)."
[ES] En ERC 3-5D, mantener bicarbonato sérico en 24-26 mmol/L.

**6.2.1** "In adults with CKD 3-4 not taking active vitamin D analogs, we suggest that a
total elemental calcium intake of 800-1,000 mg/d (including dietary calcium, calcium
supplementation, and calcium-based phosphate binders) be prescribed to maintain a neutral
calcium balance (2B)."
[ES] En ERC 3-4 sin análogos activos de vitamina D, 800-1,000 mg/d de calcio elemental total.

**6.3.1** "In adults with CKD 3-5D, we recommend adjusting dietary phosphorus intake to
maintain serum phosphate levels in the normal range (1B)."
[ES] En ERC 3-5D, ajustar el fósforo dietético para mantener el fosfato sérico en rango normal.

**6.3.2** "In adults with CKD 1-5D or posttransplantation, it is reasonable when making
decisions about phosphorus restriction treatment to consider the bioavailability of
phosphorus sources (eg, animal, vegetable, additives) (OPINION)."
[ES] Considerar la biodisponibilidad de la fuente de fósforo (animal, vegetal, aditivos).

**6.4.1** "In adults with CKD 3-5D or posttransplantation, it is reasonable to adjust
dietary potassium intake to maintain serum potassium within the normal range (OPINION)."
[ES] En ERC 3-5D o postrasplante, ajustar el potasio dietético para mantener potasio
sérico en rango normal.

**6.5.1** "In adults with CKD 3-5 (1B), CKD 5D (1C), or posttransplantation (1C), we
recommend limiting sodium intake to less than 100 mmol/d (or <2.3 g/d) to reduce blood
pressure and improve volume control."
[ES] En ERC 3-5, 5D o postrasplante, limitar sodio a < 100 mmol/d (< 2.3 g/d) para reducir
presión arterial y mejorar control de volumen.

## Micronutrientes (textual, resumen de declaraciones)

**5.0.1** "In adults with CKD 3-5D or posttransplantation, it is reasonable for the
registered dietitian nutritionist (RDN) or an international equivalent to encourage eating
a diet that meets the recommended dietary allowance (RDA) for adequate intake for all
vitamins and minerals (OPINION)."
[ES] Fomentar una dieta que cubra la RDA de todas las vitaminas y minerales.

**5.6.1** "In adults with CKD 1-5D, we suggest to not routinely supplement selenium or zinc
since there is little evidence that it improves nutritional, inflammatory, or micronutrient
status (2C)."
[ES] No suplementar rutinariamente selenio ni zinc sin deficiencia demostrada.
